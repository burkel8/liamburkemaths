Research

I work in numerical linear algebra. In particular, I develop Krylov subspace methods.

My research output is listed below.

[1] Burke, L, Frommer, A., Ramirez G. H, Soodhalter, K. M., Krylov Subspace Recycling For Matrix Functions, in arXiv:2209.14163 [math.NA] (2022) link

[2] Burke, L, Unprojected Recycled Block Krylov subspace methods for shifted systems, in arXiv:2209.06922 [math.NA] (2022) link

My masters thesis involved developing a template C++ class for simulations of scalar field theory and the Ising Model on a 3 dimensional lattice. This thesis can be found here.
